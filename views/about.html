About

Purpose

Crowdfix is designed to help the community by taking input from city locals and allowing them to vote on an idea. The concept is all about making it easier to bring smaller scale issues to light and using the community to provide practical solutions as efficiently as possible.

Community Involvement

With Crowdfix, community involvement is maximized. Users can post ideas that they think should be implemented to not just benefit the city but also its residents. Citizen collaboration greatly fosters improved community values and should immensely benefit your city.

City Officials

This platform is also designed to integrate input/feedback from government and city officials with community opinions. When city officials support a certain idea, it will be visible to all site members and this would speed up community support for that idea. City officials can stay on top of community requests to improve the city and can take active positions on many of these ideas which will improve communication between the citizens and government/city officials.
